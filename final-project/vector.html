My inspiration for this project is Shinto Buddhism. Shinto Buddhists believe that God can be found in everything, even in technology. To highlight this union of the spirtual and modern world, I created a Shinto Buddhist dwelling in an urban setting. I created this character wearing the symbol of this dharma as a hairpin using Illustrator; the background image of a dissolving city I found on the internet. The quote is a proverb from this tradition - a reminder to all that life is but a dream.
