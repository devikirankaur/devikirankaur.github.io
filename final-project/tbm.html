My inspiration for this project intially began as a question: WHY DID THE COW JUMP OVER THE MOON? I received the answer in the form of a download from the univserse - "to die." Although it felt morbid at the time, I sat with it until the full message came through which is conveyed by this animation tells. The tag line "putting the 'carne' in reincarnation" was inspired by a beloved friend after I told him the story line. In creating this animation, my prayer is that it inspires humans to practice more compassion toward animals.
